About Us

Welcome to M & A Tours and Travels, your trusted travel companion in Sri Lanka. Based in the beautiful coastal town of Hiriketiya, Dikwella, we specialize in providing exceptional transportation services and unforgettable travel experiences across this paradise island.

With years of experience and local expertise, we pride ourselves on offering personalized service, comfortable vehicles, and knowledgeable drivers who know Sri Lanka inside out. Whether you're planning a beach getaway, mountain adventure, or cultural exploration, we're here to make your journey smooth and memorable.
